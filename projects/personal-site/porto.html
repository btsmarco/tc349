Marco Botros is studying Computer Science Engineering in Michigan State University. He has passion to the limitless creativity available in Computer Science. Marco is still discovering the vast industry but, he had shown special interest to web development and mobile development. Although he is open to discover new fields, he enjoys the direct relationship with customers in these two fields. Marco works as a Web Developer and Designer in the State News which builds and maintains more than twelve newspaper websites. Marco has also made a Windows phone application called “Agpeya” which is a prayer book serving the Coptic (Christian Egyptian) community. Marco is a Coptic Orthodox Christianity, he is very passionate about his religion. Marco also enjoys reading and photography.

According to Gallup’ Strengths quest, two of Marco’s strengths are Achiever which gives him more stamina and drive to achieve his tasks and Learner which allows Marco to eagerly learn continuously which is very crucial in this industry. On the other hand, Marco is a co-founder and vice-president of Generate Help 2 Heal Generations, which is a student run organization which sends medical supplies to countries in need. Marco is also a Scout Leader, he has been a part of the Egyptian Federation for Scouts for more than 12 years.
