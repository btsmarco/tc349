VOLUNTEER TIME

If you would like to volunteer, write us at ghtohg@gmail.com you can help sort, categorize and prepare medical supplies for shipment at our warehouse in East Lansing.

We categorize and sort medical supplies every Friday from 1:00 PM to 6:30 PM, tell us when you can come to help.

DONATE SUPPLIES

If you would like to donate supplies, write us at ghtohg@gmail.com we accept all kind of medical supplies such as walkers, crutches , surgical instruments, beds, tools, gloves and gowns, wheelchairs, etc. We will appreciate these greatly.

TELL YOUR HOSPITAL

If you have contacts at your local hospital, please reach out to them on our behalf and write us at ghtohg@gmail.com.

Tell your local hospital that we accept unused, unexpired medical supplies and equipment, as well as sterile surgical supplies such as intravenous lines, syringes and sutures./p>
