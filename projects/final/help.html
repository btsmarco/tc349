Volunteer

If you would like to volunteer, write us at ghtohg@gmail.com you can help sort, categorize and prepare medical supplies for shipment at our warehouse in East Lansing.

We categorize and sort medical supplies every Friday from 1:00 PM to 6:30 PM, tell us when you can come to help.

"Unless someone like you cares a whole aweful lot, nothing is going to get better. It's not" Dr. Seuss

Donate

If you would like to donate supplies, send to us an email at ghtohg@gmail.com. we accept all kinds of medical supplies such as walkers, crutches, surgical instruments, beds, tools, gloves and gowns, wheelchairs, etc. We will appreciate these greatly.

"I've learned that you shouldn't go through life with a catchers mitt on both hands. You need to be able to throw something back." Maya Angelou

Tell Your Hospital

If you have contacts at your local hospital, please reach out to them on our behalf and write us at ghtohg@gmail.com.

Tell your local hospital that we accept unused, unexpired medical supplies and equipment, as well as sterile surgical supplies such as intravenous lines, syringes and sutures.
